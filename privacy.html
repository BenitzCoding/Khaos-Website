Our Privacy Policy

Most Data is Collected for Enrollment into

  1. Staff Applications
  2. Partnership
  3. Senarc Security Lab
Other Senarc future authorizations too

What Data We Store

  1. User ID
  2. Username
  3. Discriminator
  4. Public Flags
  5. banner hash
  6. Avatar Hash
  7. Discord Banner Colour
  8. 2FA Settings
  9. Email

Used For:

  1. Notifying users via emails
  2. Identity Verification
  3. Application Troll Prevention

How is it Stored?

  1. We store these information in discord channels via webhooks. These channels are only accessable by the Founder, Administrator, and Managers.

How Does One Request to delete their data?

  1. You can request to delete your data by sending a email to the our Network Management Team or our Support Team. Make sure you provide why you want it deleted and if you have any other concerns.
  2. Network Management Team: network@senarc.org
  3. Support Team: support@senarc.org
